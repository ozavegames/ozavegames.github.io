Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 28, 2026

This privacy policy applies to all games and applications developed and published by Ozave Games. By downloading or using our games, you agree to this policy.

1. About us

Ozave Games is an independent mobile game development studio based in India. We develop and publish casual and board games for Android devices, currently available in India, Bangladesh, Pakistan, Sri Lanka, and Nepal.

2. Information we collect

Ozave Games does not directly collect, store, or process any personally identifiable information from users of our games.

However, our games use third-party services that may collect certain information automatically. These services are:

  • Google AdMob — used to display advertisements within our games

3. Google AdMob

Our games use Google AdMob to serve advertisements. AdMob may collect and use data to provide personalised or non-personalised ads. This may include:

  • Device identifiers (such as Advertising ID)
  • Device information (model, operating system version)
  • General location information (country or region level only)
  • Ad interaction data (impressions, clicks)
  • IP address

This data is collected and processed by Google in accordance with Google's own privacy policy. We do not have access to or control over this data.

You can review Google's privacy policy here: https://policies.google.com/privacy

You can opt out of personalised advertising by adjusting your device settings: Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalisation.

4. Data we do not collect

Ozave Games does not collect:

  • Your name, email address, or any contact information
  • Account credentials or passwords
  • Payment or financial information
  • Precise GPS location
  • Camera or microphone data
  • Contacts or files from your device
  • Any data from children under 13 years of age

5. Children's privacy

Our games are designed to be family friendly. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children under the age of 13. Our games comply with Google Play's Families Policy. AdMob is configured to serve non-personalised ads only in contexts where users may be children.

6. Internet permission

Our games require internet access solely for the purpose of loading and displaying advertisements through Google AdMob. The core gameplay of all our games works fully offline and does not require an internet connection.

7. Third party links

Our games may contain links to the Google Play Store for rating the app. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of third party websites or services. We encourage you to review their privacy policies.

8. Data security

Since Ozave Games does not collect or store personal data on our own servers, there is no personal data at risk from our side. Data collected by Google AdMob is secured according to Google's own security standards.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time as our games or applicable laws change. Any updates will be posted on this page with a revised "last updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
